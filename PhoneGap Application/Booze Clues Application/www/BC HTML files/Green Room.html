Green Room

Green Room has something for everyone. Whether you want to play darts, relax and socialize on the sofas or dance the night away Green Room is the place for you. Known for their Fishbowls on Wednesdays and you cannot leave without trying a nerd bucket. Equipped with two full Bars and one of the biggest dancefloors in Plattsburgh, the versatility of Green Room is unmatched. Guaranteed that you will have a great time, no matter what you are looking for.
