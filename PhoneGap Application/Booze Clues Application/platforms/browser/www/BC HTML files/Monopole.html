Monopole

Monopole is Plattsburgh's oldest bar. Established in 1898, it sets the standard for all other bars in Plattsburgh. A common watering hole for both college students and local residents. Most notibly know for Wing Wednesday and Football Sunday. With drink specials everyday and live music on the second floor, Monpole is one of Plattsburgh's premier downtown destinations.
